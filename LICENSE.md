## 🎯 What Is ProSim for Xcode Simulator?

ProSim is a companion app designed for iOS developers working with Xcode Simulator. It extends Apple’s simulator with extra tools for testing, debugging, and UI preview creation.

It is widely used by:

* iOS developers
* SwiftUI developers
* QA testers
* App designers
* Indie developers

> 💡 It is a productivity booster for anyone building iOS apps on macOS.
